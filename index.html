    Experiment 2: LLM Scene Abstraction Evaluation

    Welcome to Experiment 2! In this study, you will read a sentence describing a situation with a specific keyword and answer three questions about the keyword.

    1) Events

    What happened with the <keyword> in the situation? What did they do, or what occurred to them?

    2) Properties

    What are the prominent properties of the <keyword> in this situation? Which properties feel most meaningful?

    3) Emotions

    Which emotions or wishes does the <keyword> evoke in the situation?

    After writing your own answers, you will be shown two answers generated by a large language model. Your task will then be to:

    1. Choose which of the two model-generated answers is better.
    2. Rate how satisfied you are with the answer you chose.
    3. Identify any problems in the answer you did not choose.
